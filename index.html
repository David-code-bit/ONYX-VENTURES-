About Us

Onyx Ventures is a creative and decorative services company based in Nigeria, dedicated to bringing beauty, personality, and professionalism to every project we undertake. From exquisite frame making to bold T-shirt printing, we combine craftsmanship with creativity to deliver results that exceed expectations.

Our team of skilled artisans and designers works closely with individuals, businesses, and organisations to understand their unique needs and translate them into stunning visual experiences. We believe that quality and creativity go hand in hand, and that belief drives everything we do.

Whether you need a beautifully framed photo, a custom card for a special occasion, vibrant printed T-shirts for your team, or premium cladding for your space — Onyx Ventures is your trusted partner.

Our Services

We provide reliable and professional services to help individuals and businesses achieve their goals with creativity, quality, and excellence.

Frame Making & Sales

We specialise in making and selling high-quality frames for photos, artworks, certificates, mirrors, and wall décor. Our frames are carefully crafted with durable materials and stylish designs to suit your taste. We also offer customised framing services.

Arts & Entertainment

We provide creative arts and entertainment services designed to inspire, engage, and bring joy. From unique art pieces and decorative designs to creative events and entertainment solutions, our goal is to add beauty and lasting impressions.

Designs of Cards

We create beautifully designed cards for all occasions, including birthdays, weddings, anniversaries, and corporate events. Our custom card designs are tailored to your style and message, ensuring each piece is unique, creative, and memorable.

Printing on T-Shirts

We print all kinds of custom graphics and photos on T-shirts — birthday pictures, personalised artwork, team uniforms, and more. High-quality print that lasts, in any style you choose. We look forward to building a long-lasting partnership with you.

Cladding

Premium cladding solutions that enhance and protect any interior or exterior surface. We work with a range of materials to deliver durable, attractive finishes for residential, commercial, and event spaces.
